Impact

Through ongoing R&D, Novamont's material scientists developed Mater-Bi, a fully biodegradable and compostable biopolymer made from corn starch and other renewable raw materials.
As a market-leading bioplastic, Mater-Bi combines the versatility of conventional plastics with environmental benefits. [4a]

It is being used in the manufacturing of a wide range of products including: [4a]

  • Bags
  • Packaging
  • Tires
  • Toys
  • Disposable Diapers
  • Mulching Film

What this means

Catia Bastioli's pioneering work in bioplastics has had a lasting impact on industry and society. Through Mater-Bi, a family of plant-based and biodegradable polymers, she provided a sustainable alternative to traditional plastics. Mater-Bi helps preserve the environment by reducing pollution, improving soil fertility, lowering greenhouse gas emissions and decreasing both pesticide and water use. By combining eco design and innovative chemistry, Bastioli demonstrates how science and entrepreneurship can tackle plastic pollution, soil degradation and climate change.
